Ontario Schools and Office Building Design

During my work term at WalterFedy, I worked on a few different projects, and concentrated on school and office design.

Timeline: 4 Months
Team: Various teams throughout the term
Software: AutoCad, Sketchup, V-ray, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign
Roles: Creator of 3D models, designer, site and code analysis/research

My Role

Early on in my term, I had the opportunity to lead the design on a multi-classroom renovation and conversion to a special education centre. This involved research and design into student needs, and careful considerations for feasibility with limited space and funds for the conversion. My design was approved, and after my term ended, the space was constructed.

Additionally, I provided support on other school projects by creating design options using software such as AutoCad and Sketchup. In order to create the options, I considered student circulation, budgetary requirements, and visual design principles.

For second half of my term, I worked on an office building with strong time constraints, since the building was to provide necessary space for aa company to grow. My role in the project included developing visualizations of design options, refining drawings sets, and creating material palette study boards.

I also worked on a 24 hour design competition with the firm where we envisioned how the journey up Mount Everest could be more sustainable through small scale interventions.
